Data Science with Python

Dive deep into machine learning, data visualization, and statistical analysis. Work with real datasets and industry tools.

Taught by: Anjali Gupta | 16 Weeks | Advanced Level

Course Overview

Total Duration: 16 Weeks

Level: Advanced

Prerequisites: Python programming & basic statistics

Projects: 5 Real-world case studies

Tools: Python, Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn, Scikit-learn, TensorFlow

Curriculum Breakdown

  • Week 1-2: Python for Data Science (NumPy, Pandas, Data Cleaning)
  • Week 3-4: Exploratory Data Analysis & Visualization (Matplotlib, Seaborn)
  • Week 5-6: Statistical Analysis & Hypothesis Testing
  • Week 7-8: Feature Engineering & Dimensionality Reduction
  • Week 9-10: Machine Learning Fundamentals (Supervised & Unsupervised Learning)
  • Week 11-12: Advanced ML Algorithms (Ensemble Methods, SVMs)
  • Week 13-14: Neural Networks & Deep Learning Basics (TensorFlow/Keras)
  • Week 15-16: Model Deployment & Capstone Project

Projects You'll Complete

  • Predictive Modeling for Business Analytics
  • Natural Language Processing (NLP) Application
  • Time Series Forecasting Project
  • Computer Vision Mini-Project
  • End-to-End Data Science Capstone
