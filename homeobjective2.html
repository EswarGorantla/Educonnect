Course Overview

  • Week 5: Prototyping & Interaction Design
  • Week 6: Usability Testing & Design Iteration
  • Week 7: Responsive Design & Design Systems
  • Week 8: Portfolio Development & Design Career Tips

What You'll Create

  • Website Redesign with Improved User Flow
  • Interactive Dashboard with Data Visualization
  • Professional Design Portfolio
